About us

After having an excess of high-end bags she didn’t know what to do with as she was no longer wearing them; Reem Hefny decided to start Awesome Blossom in 2012. An Instagram page that filled the market gap by selling designer bags that people no longer need at a lower price and with the best quality. Offering the best of both worlds to buyers and sellers!
